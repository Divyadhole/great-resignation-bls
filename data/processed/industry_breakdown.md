# Great Resignation: Industry Breakdown

## Quit Rates by Industry (Peak 2021)
| Industry | Peak Quit Rate | vs 2019 Baseline | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Accommodation & Food Services | 5.7% | +2.1 ppts | Hardest hit |
| Retail Trade | 4.4% | +1.6 ppts | Very high |
| Arts, Entertainment & Recreation | 4.1% | +1.4 ppts | High |
| Health Care & Social Assistance | 3.2% | +0.9 ppts | Elevated |
| Manufacturing | 2.8% | +0.7 ppts | Moderate |
| Finance & Insurance | 1.8% | +0.4 ppts | Low |
| Federal Government | 0.6% | +0.1 ppts | Minimal |

**Source:** BLS JOLTS Industry Data — JTS0000000000000000QUR

## Key Dates
- April 2021: Great Resignation officially begins (quit rate breaks 2.5%)
- November 2021: Peak quit rate of 3.0% nationally
- December 2022: Quit rate returns to pre-pandemic baseline range
- July 2023: Confirmed return to 2.0% "normal" range
