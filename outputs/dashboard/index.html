Jan 2019 – Dec 2023
Key Finding: Between April 2021 and December 2022, American workers quit their jobs at rates never seen in recorded US history. The quit rate peaked at 3.0% in November 2021 — +19.7% above the pre-pandemic baseline. Statistical test: Welch t = 12.1, p < 0.001, Cohen's d = 3.82 (large effect). The Great Resignation was real, lasted 20 months, and returned to normal by July 2023.
